Jaber Valinejad 

I received my PhD degree and master degree in computer science from Virginia Tech, Washington DC, USA. He is currently a postdoctoral researcher in the Data and System Science lab at Harvard University’s Medical School in Cambridge, MA, USA. He was a fellow of the following NSF-sponsored interdisciplinary programs while pursuing the PD degree at Virginia Tech: (1) disaster resilience and risk management (DRRM); and (2) urban computing. His research focuses on community resilience, computational social science, energy, and socio-technical systems through the use of network science, data science, natural language processing (NLP), social sensing tools (such as Twitter and Google), machine learning, and optimization techniques. In recent years, my work has been supported by the European Union’s Horizon 2020 Research, the National Science Foundation, COMPETE 2020, Khalife University, and the British Council.
